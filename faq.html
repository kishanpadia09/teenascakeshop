FAQs

How can I place an order for my wedding cake/hampers/gifts?

Step 1 - get in touch as soon as you know your date

Step 2 - we plan and design your order that fits with your event size, style and budget

Step 3 - make your booking fee payment - you will receive an order confirmation

Step 4 - we will have a final run through over email or by phone where any changes can be made

Step 5 - delivery of your beautiful bespoke cake!



How much will it cost?

It’s all in the details, all cakes and sweet treats are completely unique, this means there are so many
variables to consider when pricing your cake and other wedding goodies. It can depend on
shape, size, how many servings, location along with the style, finish and level complexity.
(minimum orders do apply)



I have seen a cake online!

That’s great, there are some incredible designs out there and we can certainly use it as our starting point.
We love the design process and using it as a reference, making slight adjustments and tweaks to the
design will allow it to become more personal to you.



When should I place an order?
It really is best to get in touch as soon as you know your event date to check availability, even if you’re not
sure of your exact requirements. Some months especially during April-May and December-February have
limited availability due to peak season and we do get lots of enquires for last minute orders. We will do our
best to fulfil them where possible.



I have no idea what I would like!

Don’t worry, this happens, a lot! From experience we are always able to come up with an initial design that
may even evolve into something completely different. I will talk you through all the options and what
may seem daunting is actually really fun, honestly!



What size do I need?

This is usually determined by the number of guests you need to cater for and how you would like to serve the
cake. Some stay with traditional size portions whilst others would like to serve the cake as dessert. Another
consideration is how you would like the cake to be visually. If you only need a small cake but wanted the
look of a grander cake, we could introduce false tiers or alter the size and height of the real cake.

Each cake can be altered to fit any size event. For example a three tiered cake would typically be 8 kgs
and could cater for 80 guests
